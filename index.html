Telephony in the Cloud

Sillyio is an open-source Adhearsion application for voice communications that implements all of Twilio's APIs. Instead of using Twilio, you can launch your own Amazon EC2 image in seconds. Yes, we just re-wrote their entire company and open sourced it. Silly, huh?
